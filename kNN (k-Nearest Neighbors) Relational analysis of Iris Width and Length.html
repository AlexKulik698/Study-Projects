Accuracy of K conclusion¶

As can be seen from my graph as the number of K increases the less accurate the model becomes, the reason for it is because we are using a broader spectrum on our learning sample reaching for further examples in our training data set which might not be fully related. If our sample was larger this number would be further diluted. It is best to keep K at a lower range of numbers to maintain the accuracy of our predictions. However having K value too low will result in a lower accuracy as well as there are fewer neighbors chosen to train the predictions from, it is ideal to keep K at 3-5 to get the best results.
